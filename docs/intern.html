Social Media Marketing Intern

EFI Connection

Week 1-5

I began my internship on December 18th, 2023. In the the first five weeks I've been here, I have become acquainted with all the people and processes that happen here. EFI Connection is a wire harness shop that specializes in GM TP1, LT1, and LS-Series V8 engines. Other branches include EFI Conversion, BenchForce, and LS-Wiring. My job is to monitor the socials of these businesses/sites and create content for them. I am also taking videos and photos for the EFI Connection products. Ultimately, during these weeks, I have been taking footage, scheduling posts, and studying the parts we sell.

After this first inital post, I will provide weekly updates to this blog page.

Week 6

During week six, I have been organizing blog posts to schedule on two of the company sites. These blog posts talk about current industry products and car information. We had a meeting about Outreach Projects on Friday to figure out how we should increase our apperance amongst car enthusiaists. I also brainstormed ideas for how to increase reach through our BenchForce and EFI Connection site. Futhermore, I am looking into a content scheduler for all of our socials, so posting content and social analytics can be easier to access.
